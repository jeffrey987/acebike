SPECIFICATIONS

ULTRA SMOOTH INNER WALL
We use a specially shaped bladder in moulding the rims. Comparing with traditional bladder, it’s designed in a correct size and shape to support the rim’s inside correctly & smoothly. The result is that we could see a very clean and smooth inner wall, which means the carbon fabrics are strentched smoothly and optimized, which ensure the best strength of the carbon fabrics.
