MORE INFORMATION
G25C-AS27-650B DISC brake wheelset is born for gravel bikes in mountains with a 27mm internal width. You could simply mount MTB tires and ride into the mountains to explore the nature and fun.
Since this wheel is with a hookless bead design for a better impact strength, we recommend to mount tubeless tires and within maximum 60PSI tire pressure.

ULTRA SMOOTH INNER WALL
We use a specially shaped bladder in moulding the rims. Comparing with traditional bladder, it’s designed in a correct size and shape to support the rim’s inside correctly & smoothly.
The result is that we could see a very clean and smooth inner wall, which means the carbon fabrics are strentched smoothly and optimized, which ensure the best strength of the carbon fabrics.
